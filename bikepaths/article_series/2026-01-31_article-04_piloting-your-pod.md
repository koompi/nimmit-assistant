### Piloting Your Pod: How Humans and AI Work Together

At Nimmit, *artificial intelligence* makes your team ten times faster and much more accurate. We solve problems by creating a *virtual pod*. This is a small team where humans and systems work together in a perfect loop.

### The 80/20 Rule of Work

In almost every job, there are two types of tasks:
1.  **Friction**: The boring, repetitive work like formatting documents, searching for files, or checking spelling.
2.  **Judgment**: The smart work like making creative choices, understanding a client's feelings, or solving a unique problem.

In a traditional business, humans spend 80% of their time on *friction* and only 20% on *judgment*. This is why people feel tired and stressed, and why mistakes happen. In a *virtual pod*, we flip this. Our systems handle the *friction* so that our human experts can focus entirely on *judgment*.

### You are the Pilot

When you work with Nimmit, your role as a manager of people is no longer the focus of your job. You become the *pilot* of your business. 

Think of a modern airplane. The computer handles the millions of small adjustments needed to stay in the air. The pilot's job is to make the big decisions: where to go and how to land safely. 

This is how we use *ai-powered teams*. Your *virtual pod* uses our *context cloud* to handle the details, but you provide the final *judgment*. This is how one person can achieve the results of a twenty-person company.
